About Deck:

The vision behind the Mysturreal Tarot Deck is rooted in the belief that within each of us lies a rich tapestry of light and shadow, beauty and mystery, power and grace. We are a harmonious blend of these forces, and by embracing them, we awaken our true potential. When we focus our thoughts, words, and actions on the radiant aspects of positivity, we ignite the power within to manifest our dreams and shape a brighter world—not only for ourselves but for all those whose lives we touch. In this balance of energies, we discover the magic that connects us to the universe and to one another. There are 78 cars in total, 22 from major arcana, and 14 cards in each of the four minor arcana suits.

About Major Arcana:

The Major Arcana comprises 22 enigmatic cards, each a gateway to profound spiritual truths and pivotal life transformations. These archetypal figures—The Fool, The Magician, The Empress, and others—carry deep, esoteric meanings, guiding the seeker through a mystical journey of self-realization and awakening. In "Mysturreal," the mystically surreal art of the Major Arcana evokes a haunting beauty, where intricate imagery weaves together the essence of these ancient symbols, drawing the querent into the enlightened mystery of their own soul’s path.

About Minor Arcana:

The Minor Arcana is woven into four elemental suits: Swords, Wands, Cups, and Pentacles. Each suit whispers of distinct realms of existence, embodying the essence of life’s myriad experiences and the subtle energies that shape the soul’s journey. The mysteries of the mind, spirit, heart, and material world unfold through these sacred symbols.

Suit of Swords

The Suit of Swords governs the realm of intellect, the dance of conflict, and the weight of choices yet to be made. These cards often manifest as harbingers of trials, inner battles, and the formidable strength of the mind. In "Mysturreal," the Swords are adorned with elegance and grace, though they embody the piercing clarity and inevitable confrontations that arise when truth is laid bare.
